Message from the Director

Adeel Noshahi

The achievement of our goals lies in a mutually beneficial partnership of all concerned: parents, students, faculty and administration.

The technological developments of the past century in the fields of news, telecommunication and information technology have opened the world for us.

NGC’s guiding philosophy has been to provide young people in Pakistan the opportunity to become internationally competitive.

We believe that along with quality education, personality development is crucial. We endeavour to develop self-confidence, independent thinking and power of expression through dramatics, debates, and sports.

Adeel Noshahi
Director NGC
