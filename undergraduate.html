Undergraduate Programs

Empowering Students to Become Future Leaders & Innovators

About Undergraduate Studies

At Noshahi Group of Colleges, our undergraduate programs are designed to transform students into competent professionals. We emphasize innovation, leadership, and real-world problem solving through a modern curriculum, hands-on projects, and experienced faculty guidance.

Programs Offered

BS Computer Science

Comprehensive four-year degree covering software development, data, and AI technologies.

BS Software Engineering

Focuses on design, development, and testing of scalable software systems.

BBA (Business Administration)

Prepares future business leaders through strategic management and entrepreneurship.

BS English

Develops critical thinking, communication, and literary analysis skills.

BS Accounting & Finance

Equips students with strong analytical and financial management expertise.

BS Psychology

Understanding human behavior, cognition, and mental health with research-based learning.

BS Mathematics

Focuses on logical thinking, quantitative analysis, and mathematical modeling skills.

BS Education

Prepares students for impactful careers in teaching and educational development.

Admission Requirements

  • Intermediate / A-Levels or Equivalent Qualification
  • Minimum 50% marks required for admission
  • Copy of Result Card, CNIC / B-Form, and Passport Size Photos
  • Admission Test or Interview (if applicable)

Career Opportunities

Our graduates pursue rewarding careers in diverse industries such as IT, business, media, finance, and education. With strong theoretical foundations and practical exposure, Noshahi alumni continue to excel both nationally and internationally.
