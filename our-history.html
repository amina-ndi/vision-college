About Us

Our History

Noshahi Group Of Colleges (NGC) offers a creative and dynamic learning environment, a diverse and dedicated faculty and a student body belonging to various parts of Pakistan. NGC has a purpose-built campus with lecture halls, seminar rooms, libraries, computer labs, student common rooms and cafeterias. The campus has ample ground area for outdoor sport activities, including soccer, cricket, volleyball, and badminton.

Apart from providing rigorous academic training to its scholars, NGC also offers them an elaborate system of club activities including debate, drama, music, poetry recitation, chess, etc. A range of events and functions are organized by these clubs throughout the year.

Noshahi Group Of Colleges has been teaching for the International Programmes since 1994. NGC is a Recognised Teaching Centre of the University of London — this is the highest level of recognition and demonstrates a sustained commitment to developing excellence in teaching, student support, and administrative processes.

For almost 30 years, NGC has been preparing students for successful careers in academics, law practice, business, public service, teaching and more.

Our Philosophy

Noshahi Group Of Colleges bridges local roots with global perspectives by offering University of London programs. Through rigorous standards and diverse experiences, students access world-class education while staying in Pakistan.

State of the Art Campus

Noshahi Group Of Colleges state-of-the-art campus provides students with an enriching environment for a vibrant university life. With modern facilities, cutting-edge technology, and spacious recreational areas, students can thrive academically and socially.

Key to Success

The key to providing quality education to Noshahi Group Of Colleges students lies in fostering a dynamic learning environment, empowering dedicated educators, and leveraging innovative teaching methods to help them excel.

Mission Statement

Noshahi Developer Inc aims to deliver high-quality, innovative solutions and professional education that empower individuals and businesses to excel in the modern digital landscape.

  • Providing quality education and software solutions.
  • Encouraging innovation and personal growth.
  • Developing global competence within local culture.

Vision Statement

Our vision is to be a leading global organization, recognized for excellence in innovation, education, and technology — building a better digital future for all.

  • Inspire lifelong learning and leadership.
  • Promote creativity and technology.
  • Achieve excellence through innovation.

Graduation

The graduation ceremony at Noshahi Group Of College is a prestigious and memorable event, celebrating the academic achievements of its students. Held annually, the ceremony brings together graduates, faculty, family, and friends in a formal setting. The event features inspiring speeches from distinguished guests and alumni, the conferral of degrees, and the recognition of outstanding student accomplishments. It marks a significant milestone in the lives of graduates, symbolizing their hard work, dedication, and readiness to embark on their professional journeys. The ceremony is not only a moment of pride for the students but also a testament to the college's commitment to academic excellence.
