About Matric Program

At Vision College, our Matric Program focuses on strong conceptual learning in both Science and Arts groups. Our aim is to prepare students for higher education with confidence, discipline, and academic excellence.

The program is affiliated with the Board of Intermediate and Secondary Education, Faisalabad. Students benefit from experienced faculty, modern labs, and a supportive learning environment.
