About Matric Program

At Noshahi Group of Colleges, our Matric Program is designed to provide students with a solid foundation in science and arts subjects. Our mission is to develop critical thinking, conceptual understanding, and confidence in our learners.

The program is affiliated with the Board of Intermediate and Secondary Education, Lahore. Students can choose between Science or Arts groups, each led by experienced faculty and supported by modern learning facilities.

Admission Details

Required Documents: Matric Certificate, B-Form, Photos, and School Leaving Certificate.
