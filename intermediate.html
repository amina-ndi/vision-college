INTERMEDIATE PROGRAM

Start your journey with Vision College and build a strong foundation for your future.

Program Overview

Vision College offers Intermediate programs to help students succeed in their higher studies. Our courses combine academic knowledge with practical skills to prepare you for a bright future.

Programs Offered

F.Sc (Pre-Medical)

For students interested in medical and biological sciences.

F.Sc (Pre-Engineering)

For students aiming for engineering and technical careers.

I.C.S (Computer Science)

Learn programming, IT, and computer technologies.

F.A (Arts)

Explore creative subjects and develop critical thinking skills.

Why Choose Vision College?

Experienced and Qualified Faculty
Modern Science and Computer Labs
Merit and Need-Based Scholarships
Career Counseling and Guidance

Eligibility Criteria

  • Completed Matriculation or equivalent education
  • Minimum 50% marks for Science streams
  • Submit Result Card, B-Form, and recent photographs
  • Admissions open to both boys and girls
