Intermediate Programs

Build a Strong Foundation for Your Future with Noshahi Group of Colleges

Program Overview

Noshahi Group of Colleges offers Intermediate programs designed to prepare students for their future university studies. Our programs focus on conceptual clarity, practical skills, and academic excellence.

Programs Offered

F.Sc (Pre-Medical)

Ideal for students aiming for medical and biological science careers.

F.Sc (Pre-Engineering)

Designed for students pursuing engineering or technical fields.

I.C.S (Computer Science)

Focuses on programming, IT, and modern computing technologies.

F.A (Arts)

Offers a wide range of subjects to develop critical thinking and creativity.

Why Choose Noshahi?

Qualified & Experienced Faculty
State-of-the-Art Science & Computer Labs
Merit & Need-Based Scholarships
Career Counseling & Guidance

Eligibility Criteria

  • Matriculation or Equivalent Education
  • Minimum 50% marks for Science Streams
  • Copy of Result Card, B-Form, and Photos Required
  • Admissions open to both male and female students
